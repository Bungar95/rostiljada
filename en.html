Tips for preparing the best barbecue

Buy a good barbecue, if possible, charcoal grill. Gas grills are good, but the food won't retain that special flavour and smell. It's also a good idea to use a lid, because it will keep the temperature and the food flavour constant.
Don't grill when the flames are the strongest. Let them subside and wait until the charcoal turns gray.
Marinade every 10 minutes to prevent the meat from drying.
Add vegetables and potatoes. Cut them in thin slices and cook them without any oil or seasoning. Also, don't forget the bread and the buns.
Don't poke the meat with a fork. By doing that, the flavour juices will escape and the meat will be drier. Rather, use a spatula or tongs.

precautions

Always barbecue outside and in a big, spacious area. Watch out for low-hanging tree branches, don't wear long-sleeve T-shirts and keep flammable items away.
Set the grill on a flat surface to prevent it from tipping over.
Never leave the grill unattended.
Be prepared in case of a fire – equip yourself with baking soda, a bucket of sand or a fire extinguisher.
If you have a gas grill, make sure to always check the gas for possible leaks.

About

Nice sunny days are here, and with them one of our favourite outdoor activities – barbecue. Delicious food, good music, and good company are the essential ingredients for a perfect barbecue and guaranteed fun. Here on this website you can find the most important information about Studentska roštiljada, outdoor barbecue organized by the Student Union of the University of Osijek. It's always accompanied by concerts of famous bands, free of charge. You can check out the gallery of barbecue's photos from previous years. There are also some useful tips for preparing the best barbecue, precautions, and a list of ideal locations for outdoor barbecue in Osijek. Come and have fun!
